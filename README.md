# E-commerce Analytics Dashboard (Power BI + ZoomCharts)

This project analyzes sales, customer behavior, product performance, pricing patterns, and loyalty for a global software retailer offering subscriptions and add-ons across Analytics, AI, Collaboration, and Design product lines.

It is built as a **3-page interactive Power BI report** enhanced with **ZoomCharts Drill-Down visuals** for deep, intuitive exploration.

---

## 📌 Project Objectives
- Track sales and loyal-customer growth over time  
- Identify high-performing channels and acquisition sources  
- Understand how discounts influence repeat buying  
- Reveal top-selling products and customer value drivers  
- Analyze refund patterns, pricing differences, and regional performance  
- Determine attach-rate between core products and add-ons  
- Support consumer transparency and business decision-making  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Structure

### **Page 1 — Sales Performance & Revenue Drivers**
Focuses on revenue trends, channel performance, product breakdown, and geographic contribution.
- KPIs: Net Revenue, Loyal Customers, Total Orders, Refund Rate  
- Timeline series for monthly revenue trends  
- Product hierarchy breakdown (billing cycle → category → product)  
- Channel & payment method contribution  
- Revenue map by country  

---

### **Page 2 — Customer Segmentation & Loyalty**
Explores who the customers are and what drives repeat purchasing.
- KPIs: Avg Revenue/Order, Avg Revenue/Customer, Loyal Customers, Total Revenue  
- Loyal customers by acquisition channel (segment/region/age-band toggle)  
- Cohort timeline: loyalty by signup month  
- Loyalty by product category → product → version  
- Currency preference breakdown  

---

### **Page 3 — Retention, Refunds & Pricing Intelligence**
Investigates repeat-purchase timing, refund behavior, discount effectiveness, and pricing variance.
- KPIs: Avg Revenue/Customer, Days to Second Purchase, ASP, Refund Amount & Count  
- Time-to-second-purchase by customer & channel  
- Discount codes vs sales & loyalty  
- Revenue per customer by product + billing cycle  
- Refund reasons analysis  
- Avg selling price by region & currency  

---

## 🧮 Key DAX Measures
The report uses custom measures for:
- Loyal customer identification  
- Monthly cohort loyalty tracking  
- Average days to second purchase  
- Refund rate, refund amount, and refund reason metrics  
- ASP (avg selling price) by currency & region  
- Attach-rate between core products and add-ons  

---

## 🗂 Dataset Overview
Includes:
- Customer demographics  
- Product details (category, subscription type, billing cycle)  
- Transaction events (orders, invoices, renewals, refunds)  
- Channels, currencies, payment methods  
- Pricing, FX conversions, and promotions
